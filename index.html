Cloud Business Unit — Cycle Time & Efficiency

Focused statistical analysis of the Cloud unit's sales performance. Examines cycle time dispersion between open and closed opportunities, applies outlier removal to isolate typical behavior, and investigates whether longer cycles correlate with higher deal value — broken down by opportunity type and acquisition channel.

Python Plotly Outlier Removal Scatterplot Heatmap Channel Attribution
Key finding
Open opportunities show shorter cycles than closed ones, but high dispersion required outlier removal for reliable interpretation. Heatmap and scatterplot tested whether cycle length predicts deal value, segmented by opportunity type.
